# GPT-5 Context

## What is Doomsday Explorer?

It is **not** primarily a software project.

Software, websites, simulations and AI integrations are implementation details.

The project explores interaction between humans, AI and games. It mixes engineering, research, HCI, simulation and storytelling.

"Fun & Profit" is the primary entry point. The goal is to motivate exploration rather than lecture users.

---

## How should GPT-5 approach this project?

Assume there is an intentional design behind unusual decisions.

Before suggesting simplifications, ask whether something exists because of:

* gameplay
* AI interaction
* HCI research
* immersion
* experimentation

rather than because it is unfinished.

---

## What should GPT-5 be good at?

GPT-5 is expected to contribute primarily through:

* architecture reviews
* documentation
* explanation of complex ideas
* project consistency
* UX and HCI feedback
* onboarding improvements
* identifying assumptions and edge cases

Coding is useful, but not the primary value.

---

## Repository philosophy

Repositories are not independent products.

Treat them as parts of one evolving ecosystem.

The website explains ideas.

The code demonstrates ideas.

The AI discusses ideas.

The game lets people experience ideas.

---

## Documentation philosophy

Documentation is written for both humans and AI.

Intent is often more important than implementation.

Understanding "why" is usually more valuable than describing "what."
